Instructions:
  • Select the difficulty level of the game.

  • No. of dashes shows the no. of letters in the words.

  • Enter a letter of your choice in the letter box & click on GUESS.

  • The total limit of uncorrect letters is 6.

  • You can autoguess a word directly but it decreases your chances. Twist is, you may not even get a word but your chance will decrease. CLICK AT YOUR OWN RISK !!

  • Clicking on HINT displays a hint of the word.
    But it decreases your incorrect limit of letters by 1.

  • You can restart the game anytime.
